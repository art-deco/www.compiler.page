Babel — Gone.

No more Babel. Through years, the industry has been transformed into a monoculture where everybody has only one solution. Such monopoly leads to unfair distribution of resources and inadequate development of the scene. By increasing the competition, we're able to challenge the "web standards church" and explore other states of Art.
